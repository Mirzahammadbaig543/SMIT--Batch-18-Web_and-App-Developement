Fruits and their advantages

Name of Fruits

  1. Banana
  2. Mango
  3. Apple

Advantages of Fruits

  • Banana
    • Rich in potassium, which supports heart health and muscle function.
    • High in dietary fiber, aiding in healthy digestion and gut health.
  • Apple
    • Provides a good source of fiber, which helps with digestion and feelings of fullness.
    • Contains antioxidants like quercetin that help protect the body's cells
  • Mango
    • Packed with Vitamin C, essential for a strong immune system.
    • Rich in Vitamin A (from beta-carotene), which is crucial for good vision.
